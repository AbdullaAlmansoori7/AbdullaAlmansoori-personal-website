About Me

Hello! I’m Abdulla Almansoori, and Jiu Jitsu has become a huge part of my life. I have been training in Jiu Jitsu for several years and have had the honor of participating in multiple competitions. The discipline, strategy, and mental toughness required in Jiu Jitsu push me to become the best version of myself both on and off the mats. Through this website, I aim to share my passion for this sport, some of my personal experiences in competitions, and what I’ve learned along the way.
